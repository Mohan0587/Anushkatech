Automatic Pump Controller With Timer










Key Features:

  • Level Indicator: Displays the current water levels for accurate monitoring and convenience.
  • Dry Run Protection: Safeguards the pump from damage by automatically halting operation during water scarcity.
  • Buzzer System: Alerts users with sound notifications for key status changes, such as low water levels or full tank capacity.
  • All Indicators: Provides clear visual signals for power, pump status, water levels, and other operational modes.
  • Automatic Operation: Controls water flow based on tank levels, ensuring uninterrupted supply without overflow or wastage.
  • Robust Design: Engineered for long-lasting performance and compatibility with various pump types and tank capacities.
  • Energy Efficiency: Minimal power consumption makes it eco-friendly and cost-effective.
  • Easy Setup: User-friendly installation and intuitive controls simplify operation.
